## Photos and recordings at events

Our events may be photographed or recorded for community, documentation, or promotional purposes (for example on Meetup, social media, or community platforms).

- By attending an event, you acknowledge that photos or videos may be taken.
- If you do **not** wish to appear in photos or recordings, please let one of the organizers know during the event. We will do our best to accommodate your request.
