Microsoft Fabric User Group Austria: Fabric, Power BI and more!

A community for anyone working with Microsoft’s data tools, including Fabric, Power BI, and related services in the Azure ecosystem. Share your knowledge, discuss new features, and connect with others using the Microsoft Data Platform in Austria. All experience levels welcome!

[Join us on LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/groups/15071006/) to share general questions, tips and tricks. You can also find the photos and slides of our previous Meetups there.

## Code of Conduct

Our user group is a community built on learning, sharing, and mutual respect. We aim to create a welcoming, inclusive, and safe environment for everyone, regardless of background, experience level, or role.

By participating in our events, online spaces, or discussions, you agree to follow this Code of Conduct.

### 1. Be respectful and inclusive

Treat all participants with respect and professionalism.

- Be considerate, welcoming, and supportive
- Value different viewpoints, backgrounds, and experience levels
- Use inclusive language and engage in constructive discussion
- Harassment, discrimination, or exclusionary behavior of any kind is not tolerated.

### 2. Keep discussions relevant and constructive

Our events and discussions focus on Microsoft Fabric, Power BI, the Microsoft data platform, and related analytics and data technologies.

- Stay on topic during sessions and discussions
- Ask questions and share opinions in a constructive manner
- Respect speakers’ time and differing technical approaches

### 3. Knowledge sharing over promotion

This community exists to exchange knowledge and real-world experience.

- Talks and discussions should prioritize learning and insight
- Sales pitches, lead generation, or excessive self-promotion are not permitted
- Vendor or product mentions should be educational and transparent

### 4. Zero tolerance for harassment

The following behavior is not acceptable at any community event or online space:

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory or derogatory comments based on gender, identity, ethnicity, religion, disability, or experience level
- Disruptive behavior during talks or discussions
- Unwelcome sexual attention or inappropriate language

### 5. Community-first mindset

This is an Austria-wide community with hybrid, remote and in-person events in Vienna, Graz, and Linz.

- Respect both in-person and remote participants
- Support speakers, organizers, and volunteers
- Help foster a friendly and collaborative atmosphere

### 6. Reporting and issue resolution

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct:

- Contact the group leader or co-leader directly, or
- Reach out privately via the official Meetup or community channels
- All reports will be handled discreetly and respectfully
- Organizers may take appropriate action, including warnings or removal from events and banning via the Meetup Platform, to protect the community.

### 7. Photos and recordings at events

Our events may be photographed or recorded for community, documentation, or promotional purposes (for example on Meetup, social media, or community platforms).

- By attending an event, you acknowledge that photos or videos may be taken.
- If you do **not** wish to appear in photos or recordings, please let one of the organizers know during the event. We will do our best to accommodate your request.
