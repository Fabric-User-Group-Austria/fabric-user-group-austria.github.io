Welcome to the Microsoft Fabric User Group Austria! 💚

Our community revolves around Microsoft Fabric, Power BI, and all things data, with the goal of sharing knowledge, learning from each other, and growing together through exciting projects.

Our Meetups take place regularly in Vienna, Graz, and Linz, and are held in a hybrid format, so you can join in person or online. Expect hands-on sessions, live demos, networking, and plenty of inspiration around Microsoft’s data platform.

➡️ You can find all upcoming events and registrations here: <https://www.meetup.com/microsoft-fabric-user-group-austria/>

We’re always happy to welcome new members, speakers, and partners who want to actively shape the community. 
If you’re interested in giving a talk or have topic suggestions → feel free to reach out!

New speakers are especially welcome!
	
If it’s your first time speaking, don’t worry - just get in touch with us. We’ll gladly help you prepare and find the right format for your talk.
